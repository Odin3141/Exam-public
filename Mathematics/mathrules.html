Academia's  Mathematics Test

Please, take your time to read the following instructions. Some of them will not be repeated!

Nature of Questions
  • The test is divided into three sections: Multi-choice, Subjective and Essay.
  • Each section is different from the others in the format of questions, format of answers and time allocation.
  • The time allocated to the Multi-choice section is thirty(30) minutes. You are required to answer all questions in this section. There are thirty(30) questions in total. You are strictly allowed to select only one answer of the four options available per question. All questions here carry equal mark
  • The time allocated to the Subjective section is thirty(30) minutes. You are required to answer all questions in this section. There are thirty(30) questions in total. You will be required to type in your answer. All questions carry different marks and this depends on how many answers you are asked to provide. Note that the answer you input must be strictly a number. Decimal, fractions and integers are allowed. Degree symbols, greek alphabets or currency signs are not allowed. Figures that are recurring should be rounded up to three significant figures, unless you are told otherwise
  • The time allocated to the Essay section is one(1) hour. You are required to answer all six questions in Compulsory Module and four out of six questions in Optional Module. There will be textboxes provided for inputting your final answer. Your final answer here will be exactly what it is on the paper. This means it may contain degree signs, math symbols, currency symbols etc. Note that the paper you will be working on will be divided into articles that will bear a question number. You must strictly show all workings in these articles and your final answer must be clearly distinguished from the other workings by circling it or underlining it. The workings you show will affect your total score so operate as directed. All questions in the Compulsory Module carry equal marks. All questions in the Optional Module carry different marks.

Test Tools
The test site has many tools to help you with your examination
  • The timer tool on the right corner shows the time you have left. You can hide the timer by clicking on it. However, it will become unhidden when there's five minutes left for the test to end. Your test will automatically be submitted onve the time is up
  • The directions tool will display how to answer questions on each section and module in case you have forgotten
  • The eliminate tool will allow you to cancel out two questions you believe are wrong. We will not see this action and the options you cancel out will be hidden.
  • The review tool will allow you to go to questions you wish to review
  • The mark tool will mark a question so that you can return to it whenever you wish

Test Rules
There are important rules that must be strictly followed during the course of the exam.
  • You should never in anyway attempt to access the questions or answers prior to the exam. If you come across these questions, you should report to your proctor.
  • The test must be taken alone without any external assistance. The camera and microphone of your device will be activated and will record until your exam has been submitted.
  • You must never attempt to view the source code of the site. If you do, the test will automatically submit
  • You should never leave the exam site for any reason.
  • No other application should be opened before and during the test.
  • You are allowed to move out of the room during breaks. Once the breaks are over, you can resume testing.
  • If you believe that there was an error, contact your proctor immediately
